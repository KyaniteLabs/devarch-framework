# Strategy Sections Reference

Required sections for the go-to-market strategy report.

## Mandatory Sections (10)

1. **Executive Summary** — 2-3 paragraph overview of product positioning and recommended next steps

2. **Market Positioning** — Where the product fits relative to alternatives, supported by development pattern evidence

3. **Pricing Analysis** — Pricing recommendations grounded in feature scope evidence from the codebase

4. **Launch Timeline** — Phased rollout plan based on development velocity and feature completeness signals

5. **Distribution Channels** — Primary and secondary channels with rationale from audience signals

6. **Growth Mechanisms** — Viral loops, referral patterns, and organic growth vectors identified from usage data

7. **Risk Assessment** — Top risks with evidence from development patterns (technical debt, single-point-of-failure, etc.)

8. **Archaeology Insights** — Unique insights from the archaeology analysis that inform strategy (this is the differentiator)

9. **90-Day Action Plan** — Concrete weekly milestones for the first quarter

10. **Success Metrics** — Measurable KPIs with targets and tracking methods

## Quality Gates

- No placeholder data — all numbers must trace to archaeology outputs
- No project leakage — zero references to other DevArch projects
- Each section must cite at least one archaeology finding as evidence
